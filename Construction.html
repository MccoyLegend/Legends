BUILDING & CONSTRUCTION

Earthworks

Earthworks is a set of activities that take place during creation of areas of level land that can be used for business, residing and recreational purposes or even roads that help ease the movement from place A to place B. Earthworks form a necessary part of construction at early stages for permanent development that is construction of foundations and building developments.


During site preparation for developments, a number of activities of earthworks takes place. These activities may expose the earth surfaces as a result of revealing, removing, disturbance or deposits of the soil/earth. Furthermore, activities such as land contouring, filling operations (cleanfills), quarrying, vegetation clearing, forestry removal, agriculture and horticulture clearing do expose the earth surfaces.


Therefore, with the aid of our experienced team we provide our customers with earthworks services which are well planned and precisely managed. Our experienced team will provide their expertise and safety measures to help avoid potential effects that may be caused by earthworks activities which may possibly harm the environment during implementation.
