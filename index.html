Company Overview

LEGENDS (PTY) LTD is a registered private company that is incorporated under the Companies Act of 2011 of the Kingdom of Lesotho. The registration number of LEGENDS PTY (LTD) is 76212. LEGENDS (PTY) LTD is a company that wishes to create business through diverse industry markets in Lesotho. Therefore, our aim is to penetrate the market through the provision of smooth myriad services. The focal services we offer comprise of Information & Technology consultancy; supply of office equipment; and Building & Construction services.
